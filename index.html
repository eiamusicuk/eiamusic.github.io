EiA

Artist, actor, model

An ethereal power-house whose theatrical roots and mixed influences spell her unique sound.

Once referred to as ‘the songbird of our generation’ EiA began her journey signing classic y. Her creativity was further ignited when she joined her school Gospel Choir and began studying Musical Theatre and by the age of 12, the artist had written her first song. EiA, who is often likened to artist Kate Bush, has seven siblings - four of whom were choristers at Westminster Abbey in London - and growing up was exposed to a whole array of music and life experiences; a driving force behind her emotion y charged writing. In 2018, while living and working as a performer in Paris, EiA began sharing her music and in 2020 softly shared her debut single, Orange, which she co-produced. She has just released her second self-produced single, My Man.
